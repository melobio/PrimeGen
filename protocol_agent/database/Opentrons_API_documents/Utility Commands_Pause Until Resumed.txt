
Call the [`ProtocolContext.pause()`](https://docs.opentrons.com/v2/new_protocol_api.html#opentrons.protocol_api.ProtocolContext.pause) method to stop a protocol at a specific step. Unlike a delay, [`pause()`](https://docs.opentrons.com/v2/new_protocol_api.html#opentrons.protocol_api.ProtocolContext.pause) does not restart your protocol automatically.
